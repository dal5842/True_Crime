Our Mission

The True Crime Text Analysis project seeks to explore crime narratives through the lens of data and language. By analyzing real cases, media coverage, and linguistic patterns, we aim to uncover deeper insights into crime reporting, bias, and storytelling in the form of a drama.

Our goal is to provide a structured, data-driven approach to understanding crime stories while fostering discussions on ethics, justice, and media representation.
