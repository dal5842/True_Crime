Website Kumu Network Analysis

Summary

The two Kumu diagrams represent structured datasets related to two different searched group of words from the episodes. Each dataset consists of common words, main characters names (first and last), the frequency of the amount of times mentioned, and the episode mentioned in. The first Kumu (word search) has a relatively simple structure, with fewer nodes and mostly direct, clear relationships, suggesting a straightforward, user-friendly website or system. The second Kumu (name search) is more complex, with more nodes and interconnected relationships, indicating a website with a more dense navigation structure, with more layers of connection between content but requiring slightly more user effort to navigate. Together, these maps highlight different levels of word frequencies.
