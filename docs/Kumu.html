Summary

The two Kumu diagrams represent structured datasets related to two different searched groups of words from the episodes. Each dataset consists of common words, main charactersâ€™ names (first and last), their frequency of mentions, and the episodes in which they appear. The first Kumu (word search) has a simpler structure with fewer nodes and direct relationships, suggesting a straightforward, user-friendly network. The second Kumu (name search) is more complex, with interconnected relationships indicating a denser navigation structure that requires slightly more effort to explore. Together, these maps highlight different levels of word and name frequencies.
